The Growing in Grace Curriculum

A Discipleship Manual for New Believers

From salvation and the Trinity to baptism, prayer, and sharing the faith — everything a new Christian needs to grow, all in one place.

What You'll Learn

Three commitments of this curriculum

Designed for the everyday Christian. Built on sound doctrine. Available to anyone who wants to grow.

Grounded in Scripture

Every lesson is rooted in the text of the Bible. No speculation, no fluff — just what Scripture actually teaches.

Practical for life

You'll learn how to apply biblical truth to daily decisions, relationships, and faith — not just abstract theology.

No prior knowledge needed

You don't need seminary training to start. Each section builds on the last, so anyone can follow from the beginning.
