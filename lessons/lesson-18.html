Lesson 18

What the Bible Actually Does in Your Life

The Bible is not just a book — it is the inspired Word of God, essential for spiritual growth. Scripture reveals God's character, transforms and renews the mind, nourishes and strengthens the soul, guards against sin, and equips every believer for every good work. To neglect God's Word is to hinder our growth; to treasure it is to flourish.
