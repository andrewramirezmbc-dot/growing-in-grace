Lesson 12

3 Ways Your Life Should Change After Meeting Jesus

Knowing Christ should transform every part of our lives. Scripture calls us to three responses: faith and repentance (receiving His gift), worship and obedience (submitting to His lordship), and proclamation and discipleship (representing Him as ambassadors in the world). He is not just Savior — He is Lord.
