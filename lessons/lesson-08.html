Lesson 08

How Can God Be One… and Three?

The word Trinity does not appear in Scripture, yet the concept is clearly taught throughout the Bible. Christianity stands firmly on monotheism — there is only one true and living God, singular in essence. And yet, this one God eternally exists as three distinct Persons. The Trinity is not a problem to be solved but a mystery to be adored.
