Lesson 14

What Does the Holy Spirit Actually Do?

The Spirit's ministry in the Old Testament was temporary and selective — empowering judges, prophets, and kings for specific tasks. But after Pentecost, the Spirit was poured out on all believers, permanently indwelling every Christian. He regenerates, seals, sanctifies, and empowers us for holy living and ministry.
