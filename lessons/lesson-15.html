Lesson 15

The Holy Spirit's Role in Your Spiritual Growth

The Holy Spirit's ongoing work in the believer is essential to spiritual growth. He convicts us of sin, sanctifies us over a lifetime, illuminates Scripture so we can understand it, and unites believers into one body. Walking in step with the Spirit is how we grow in Christlikeness.
